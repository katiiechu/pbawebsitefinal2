The Rift

This project explores the friction between the world's two largest economies when it comes to climate policy. Here, climate experts tackle the reasons for China's dominance in green energy and emissions reductions (not without addressing its challenges) and predict its future direction. We also consider that the US lags behind China in this capacity, examining why that is the case.
